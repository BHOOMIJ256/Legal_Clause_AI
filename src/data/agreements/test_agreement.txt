TRANSPORTATION SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Transportation Service Agreement (Agreement) is entered into by and between ABC Transport Company (Carrier) and XYZ Logistics (Shipper).

1. SERVICES
The Carrier shall provide transportation services for the Shipper's goods as specified in Appendix A. Services include pickup, transportation, and delivery of goods.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Payment shall be made within 30 days of receipt of invoice. Rates shall be as specified in the Rate Schedule attached as Appendix B. All charges are subject to applicable taxes and surcharges.

3. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE
Carrier shall maintain insurance coverage as specified in Appendix C. Carrier's liability for loss or damage shall be limited to the actual value of the goods or the maximum liability specified in the Agreement, whichever is less.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one year. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Early termination may be subject to termination charges.

5. SERVICE LEVELS
Carrier shall maintain service levels as specified in Appendix D. Performance metrics include delivery times, damage rates, and customer service response times.
